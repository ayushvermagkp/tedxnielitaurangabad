What is TEDx?

TEDx is more than just a conference—it's a **movement of ideas worth spreading**. It brings together **visionaries, innovators, and changemakers** from all walks of life to share **inspirational stories, groundbreaking discoveries, and transformative ideas** that push humanity forward.

Why TEDxNIELITAurangabad?

At TEDxNIELITAurangabad, we create a stage where **voices that matter** can be heard. Our mission is to **ignite curiosity, fuel creativity, and drive conversations** that inspire individuals to think differently and take action.

Our TEDx Legacy

Every TEDx event is a **journey of discovery**, where ideas have the power to **challenge, uplift, and connect** us. **Join us in shaping the future—one idea at a time.**
